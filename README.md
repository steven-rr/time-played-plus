# TimePlayed_Plus

### [Download on CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/timeplayed-plus)

A lightweight addon that tracks how long you actually play each session, with stats and history across all your characters.

---

## Why TimePlayed_Plus?

WoW's built-in `/played` command gives you a single lifetime total — a massive number that tells you almost nothing useful. TimePlayed_Plus breaks your playtime down into something meaningful: individual sessions, daily totals, weekly averages, and per-character breakdowns. And it only counts the time you're actually at the keyboard — AFK time doesn't count.

## Features

**Live Session Timer**
A real-time display of your current session. The timer color gradually shifts from green to yellow to red the longer you play — a subtle reminder when you've been at it a while.

**Daily & Weekly Stats**
See your playtime for today, your daily average, and a rolling 7-day average. Useful for understanding your play habits at a glance.

**Full Session History**
A scrollable log of every session — date, start time, duration, and which character you were on. Filter by character to see where your time is going.

**Multi-Character Tracking**
All characters feed into one unified history. View stats for a single character or see everything together.

**AFK-Aware**
Time spent AFK is automatically excluded. You see the time you were actually playing, not the time your character sat idle in Stormwind.

**Minimap Button**
Hover for a quick glance at your current session and today's total. Click to open the full window.

**CSV Export**
Export your full session history to a spreadsheet-friendly format for deeper analysis.

## Usage

- `/tpp` — toggle the main window
- `/timeplayed` — toggle the main window
- Click the minimap icon to open/close

## Installation

1. Download from [CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/wow/addons/timeplayed-plus)
2. Extract the `TimePlayed_Plus` folder into your `World of Warcraft/_retail_/Interface/AddOns/` directory
3. Restart WoW or type `/reload` if you're already logged in

## Compatibility

- WoW Retail
- WoW Classic
- WoW Cataclysm Classic
